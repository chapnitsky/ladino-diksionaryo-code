# ser

In Ladino there are two words for "to be": ser and estar

El verbo ser

Ladino                        | Inglez
----------------------------- | --------
ser                           | (to) be


## Present indicativo:

Ladino                        | Inglez
----------------------------- | --------
(yo) so                       | (I) am
(tu) sos                      | (you) are
(el/eya) es                   | (she/he) is
(mozotras/mozotros) semos     | (we) are
(vozotras/vozotros) sosh      | (you) are (pl)
(eyas/eyos) son               | (they) are
